What Is Fallout Shelter?

Fallout Shelter is a free-to-play simulation game where you play as an Overseer running your very own Vault-Tec Corporation vault. Developed by Bethesda and released in 2015, this simple yet inticing game can be run across nearly every platform - from PC, Console, to its original Mobile app incarnation. Spend your extra minutes managing a growing community that requires Power, Water, Food, and more to survive the harsh wastelands. With the includion of exploring & combat minigames that reward the player for hard effort, Fallout Shelter is a well-rounded, well-known game that stands the test of time in an ever-changing market. For more general information visit the wikipedia page or play it here.
